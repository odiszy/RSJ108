How to Dress-Up as a Journalist

Estimated time: 10 minuts
Useful Meter: 100% Effective!

While Journalism is its own thing, it’s important that you also have a good sense of style as well! Play dress up, find your groove, be creative with your look!

  • Item's You'll Need:

  • Colors
  • A style
  • Clothing Items
  • Pinterest

  1. Find colors suitable to you!

    Everyone has a color pallette that suitable to their identity or preference, so find a color that suits your personality and also compliments your skin tone.

  2. Knowing Your Body Type

    To look your best, know your body type. This can help highlight your best assets and balance out your shape. This can also help boost slef-confidence as well for your body as well!

    Body Types

  3. Clothing Styles

    When it comes to finding clothes, make sure you know the style you are looking for! What style do YOU want to try? Streetwear? Y2K? Aegyo Gyaru? Alternative? A Casual Office look? It's up to you! Pinterest has great reccommendations and inspirations for different styles.

  4. Accesories!

    Nothing compliments an outfit better than having some add ons to the fit! There are so many options, combinations and possibilities to choose from like rings, necklaces, wallet chains, hats, scarves, earrings, and more can really pull together an outfit.

  5. Bonus! What if you're on a budget?

    Thrift stores are the perfect place to shop if you're on a budget! Some places have special sales and discounts as much as 50% off clothing items. It's important to keep an open mind when thrifting, having a concept idea of your style can really help you pick out some unique pieces and amazing outfit combos to wear out!

  6. In the Layers panel, select any layer other than the base layer in the clipping mask.

    Select Layer > Release Clipping Mask.
